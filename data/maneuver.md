## Our Process

### Phase 1: Discovery (Week 1)
Every engagement starts with a founder call — not a sales call. We need to understand your business deeply: where you've been, where you're stuck, where you want to go. We talk to your team, look at your data, and challenge your assumptions. This phase has no billing.

### Phase 2: Strategy (Weeks 2–3)
We build your strategy document — not a deck, a working document. This includes diagnosis (here's actually what's going on), strategy (here's the approach), and roadmap (here's exactly what we'll do and when). You review, we refine, and we align before execution starts.

### Phase 3: Execution (Weeks 4–8)
Weekly sprints, weekly check-ins, weekly deliverables. We work inside your tools (Notion, Slack, Linear, Figma — whatever you use). Every week you see output. Every week we adjust based on what the market tells us.

### Phase 4: Scale (Ongoing)
At the end of the engagement, we hand you a fully documented, repeatable system. If you want us to stay and run it, we can. If you want to bring it in-house, we'll train your team. There's no dependency lock-in.
